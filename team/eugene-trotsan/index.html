Eugene Trotsan

Director & Technical Lead

Eugene Trotsan is Director & Technical Lead at Anthill HK. He began his professional career in Hong Kong in 2010 and has since delivered IT programmes across his native Ukraine, Europe, and Hong Kong—serving organisations in banking, logistics, government, and renewable energy.

He builds lean, dependable applications, designs network and server architectures, and leads systems integration and infrastructure work from early design through to stable production.

Eugene is fluent in English, Spanish, Russian, and Ukrainian—four human languages, before counting the programming languages that ship alongside every engagement. He enjoys multicultural teams and initiatives that span several continents and time zones.

Specialities

  • Servers & networks — architecture, hardening, and operations for business-critical workloads.
  • Logistics — platforms and integrations for complex supply-chain and movement-of-goods scenarios.
  • User interfaces — clear, efficient product surfaces that keep operators and customers aligned with the system behind them.
  • Four spoken languages — English, Spanish, Russian, and Ukrainian (excluding programming languages).

Programming stacks vary by project; the through-line is pragmatic delivery, careful integration, and systems that stay maintainable after launch.
